How does all this work?

We believe in transparent, straightforward pricing with no hidden fees or surprises. Our plans are designed to provide reliable IT support at a fair price, ensuring your business gets the help it needs without unnecessary costs. Below, you'll find answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

We understand that a subscription plan can’t cover every unique, one-time need that may arise. That’s why we also offer services on a time and materials basis, giving you the flexibility to get additional support whenever necessary.

A "user" is any employee, contracted employee, or contractor of your business who requires ongoing IT support. We do not carve out specific groups of employees when scoping a proposal—all users who need support are included in the plan.

Our plans are billed monthly per user. You’ll receive an invoice at the start of each billing cycle. If extra services outside of the standard scope of work are rendered during the month, these will be itemized on the invoice.

We don't ask for a contract for every customer. We've designed our process to be hassle-free and collaborative.
This does not apply to situations where regulations require a contract, such as with a HIPAA covered entity. In this type of situation, a business associate agreement is required, but the requirement of a written commercial contract is negotiable.

When you first sign on with us, we lock in the user count for about six months. Every six months, we will review the user count and adjust the rates accordingly.
If in the six month period you have a substantial increase in user count, we will notify you and suggest a change in billing.

For special projects, we will provide a separate estimate based on labor hours that would be spent. We don't sell equipment, but if we do source materials on your behalf for a project, we will add reimbursement to the invoice at no extra charge.
